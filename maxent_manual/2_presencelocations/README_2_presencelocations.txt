MaxENT - Step 2 presence locations of the plant (this points are derived from the 1000 genome Arabis locations of our fulgione group)

Presence locations of Arabis alpina is located in the folder (/netscratch/dep_coupland/grp_fulgione/siva/final_maxent/database_climate_presence_locations/presencepointsofarabisalpina)

The file named processed_141_presence_locations.csv is the same file as AA_joint.csv which is used in the maxent modelling and it has the total 141 unique presence locations of Arabis alpina plant across europe, these 141 locations are uniquel processed after removing the duplicates, considering one point within a raster cell within the climatic resolution of 30s ~ 1km square area.
